Day 1

Day 1 is the perfect day to experience the ancient monastery in Kyiv. The Pecherski Lavra is situated on hilly terrain that requires good shoes and around 4 hours of walking. There is alot to discover in the cemetary. In addition to sites such as ancient mummys, there are beautiful cathedrals and shops with souveniurs such as Icons and other religious craft work. The lavra has different events depending on the time of the year. In the summer, there is an annual honey festival where producers from all around Ukraine come to sell and give samples.
