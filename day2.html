Day 2

This can be a good day to take in the history of Kyiv. Begin the day by visiting the Ukrainian folk museum "Pirohovo" where you can stroll through recreations of folk villages and experience life as Ukrainians did hundreds of years ago. Afterwards, make your way to the center of modern activity, the Maidan Square also known as Independence Square. There you can experience the vibrant modern Kyiv and witness the location where the Ukrainian revolution took place.
